<Warning>This command must be configured & enabled via the /config command!</Warning>

Enabling communities for order logging management is simple, with this module, you can effortlessly share payment links to streamline order payments.

## Configuration

To configure payment links, start with running `/Config > Configure Payments.` From there, you can click from the 5 provided payment links. You may only use the max of 5, with a minimum of 1. Click on one of the numbers, and paste the link to any avatar item and or gamepass to that number. Once you have selected and pasted the amount of links you wish to use, click the green `Enable Payments` button. To use these payment links, use `/Payment-Link` and input the link number you are wishing to use, and execute the command.
